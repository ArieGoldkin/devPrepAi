Behavioral Problem Solving Leadership

Answer Framework (STAR Method)

Task: Explain your responsibility and what needed to be done
Action: Detail the specific steps you took to solve it
Result: Share the outcome and what you learned

Key Points to Include

  • ✓ Your thought process and decision-making
  • ✓ Collaboration with team members (if applicable)
  • ✓ Measurable impact and lessons learned
